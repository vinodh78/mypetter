Our Roadmap

We've got the expertise and we've got the passion. And we've got the road map. So together, we are creating progressive new initiatives while growing the programs - trap-neuter-return for community cats, shelter foster networking, online resource centers to provide neighborhood support and more - that have already brought us close to our goal.

Q1 - 2022
Jan ~ Mar

Dogs and cats at our partnering sanctuaries and in our partner lifesaving centers receive all the love, care and medical treatment they need - be it lifesaving surgery or life-changing training sessions - to find homes of their own. And because we know a home is the best place for companion animals, our partners implement programs around the country that both prevent pets from having to enter shelters in the first place and get those who do adopted more quickly.

Q2 - 2022
Apr ~ Jun

Pet Adoption : With millions of dogs and cats in shelters each year looking for new homes - and about 347,000 killed simply because they aren't chosen - there's no need to buy from a breeder or pet store. Our partner adoption agencies are committed to removing the barriers to adoption for people who want to provide a loving home for pets who need them and to ensuring the entire process is positive and accessible for everyone.
